Are you keen on doing a masters in analytics in USA?

INSOFE-BGSU collaboration provides INSOFE's students many benefits
  • An assured scholarship (up to 25% discount on the regular tuition fee) for M.S. in analytics
  • Preparation for success in the Masters program and subsequent career through a post graduate program at INSOFE (PGP in Big Data Analytics and Optimization) prior to starting the Masters in USA
"Education at Insofe and working at Icube helped me graduate from BGSU and succeed in the interview process. I am sure my experience working with you and the team (Mahesh, Shilpa, Radha Madhavi and Satish) will help me succeed in the job as well."
Jagan Mohan Kunthipuram - Data Scientist, Feedzai
  • Total cost of the program, including INSOFE's pre-masters certificate program, is less than BGSU's regular rate for a masters program. Interested students from any discipline with the following degrees can apply: B.E./B.Tech., M.E./M.Tech., M.Sc. (Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics), MCA, MBA, B.Pharm and M.Pharm.
