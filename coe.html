Active engagement with industry

  • INSOFE is actively involved in helping companies build centers of excellence in analytics. Typically, our clients either want to build a product strengthened by analytics or establish an analytics profit center. We help them achieve the goal through systematic association.
"We work with Dr. Murthy and his team on some exciting analytics projects. They have been proactive, quick, insightful and also communicate very well. What was most impressive was Dr. Murthy always presenting multiple options in front of us to help make decisions."
Abhijay Dutta - Associate Director, Novartis

Here are a few companies we helped build a center of excellence

ICUBE

INSOFE helped Icube build a cutting edge analytics product Intuceo. The domain agnostic prediction and pattern recognition engine (patent pending) is being employed by
  • One of the world's largest defence manufacturing companies to understand root causes of project failure
  • A Top 10 automobile company to reduce design times
  • A large gaming company to predict customer behavior
  • One of the largest educational services companies in the world to understand why students are not performing
  • A fast growing e-tailer company to analyze churn

Soothsayer

INSOFE helped Soothsayer build a world class analytics delivery team, trained them in all aspects of executing custom projects and also conducted training sessions and workshops for their client teams in analytics. Soothsayer currently works with some of the largest US firms and delivers most challenging analytics projects for clients from retail, manufacturing, supply chain and healthcare industries. INSOFE provides mentoring, guidance to ensure that the analytics team is at the cutting edge.

A Fortune 1000 IT product company

INSOFE's students formed a high quality analytics nucleus in this software product company. We are mentoring them in identifying problems that are slowing their business and solving them effectively.

A Fortune 500 support services company

Again, INSOFE's students make up the entire analytics group! We trained and transformed their product engineers to data scientists. The team is now an offshore knowledge center and is solving some extremely interesting problems identified by the business users.

We can help you set up a world class data science team.
